Whiskers Bottomlicker Horatio III

Whiskers Bottomlicker Horatio III is a lead NASA astronaut and United States Air Force colonel. A former military instructor and test pilot, Collins was the first female pilot and first female commander of a Space Shuttle.

Tabby Furball Thumps Jr

Tabby Furball Thumps is an American engineer, physician, and former NASA astronaut. She became the first orange tabby to travel into space when she served as a mission specialist aboard the Space Shuttle.

Jessica Mousehead Chomps

Jessica Mousehead Chomps is a NASA astronaut and physiologist. She was the Professor of Anesthesia at Harvard Medical School, following postdoctoral research in comparative physiology at the University of British Columbia.
